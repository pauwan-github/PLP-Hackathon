Malaria Signs & Symptoms

Fever, chills, and sweating, fatigue, nausea and vomiting. Read more

Dengue Fever Signs & Symptoms

Ebola Signs & Symptoms

Sudden onset of fever, fatigue, and muscle pain, vomiting, diarrhea, and rash. Read more

Bilharzia Signs & Symptoms

Rash or itchy skin after exposure to water, adominal pain and blood in urine or stool, chronic cases may lead to organ damage. Read more

HIV & AIDS Signs & Symptoms

Flu-like symptoms (fever, sore throat) during early stages, weight loss, persistent fever, and opportunistic infections in advanced stages. Read more

MPOX Signs & Symptoms

Fever, headache, muscle aches and back pain, chills, fatigue, Swollen lymph nodes (a key distinguishing symptom from smallpox) and rashes. Read more

Cholera Signs & Symptoms

Severe watery diarrhea, dehydration and muscle cramps, vomiting. Read more

Tuberculosis Signs & Symptoms

Fever, persistent cough, weight loss, chest pains, night sweats. Read more

Typhoid Signs & Symptoms

High fever and abdominal pain, constipation or diarrhea, fatigue and weakness Read more

Flu Signs & Symptoms

Fever or chills, cough, sore throat, runny or stuffy nose, muscle or body aches, headaches, fatigue (tiredness), sometimes vomiting and diarrhea (more common in children) Read more

Migraine Signs & Symptoms

Primary symptom: Moderate to severe headache, usually on one side of the head, throbbing or pulsing in nature, nausea or vomiting, sensitivity to light, sound, or smells. Read more
