Ebola Signs & Symptoms

  • Sudden onset of fever, fatigue, and muscle pain.
  • Vomiting, diarrhea, and rash.
  • Internal and external bleeding in severe cases.

Cause & Spread of Cholera

  • Caused by the Ebola virus.
  • Spread through direct contact with bodily fluids of infected individuals or contaminated objects..

Prevention & Treatment of Ebola

  • Avoid contact with infected individuals.
  • Wear protective equipment (PPE) in healthcare settings.
  • Educate communities on safe burial practices.
Supportive care (hydration, oxygen therapy) and experimental treatments like monoclonal antibodies.
