Malaria Signs & Symptoms

  • Fever, chills, and sweating
  • Fatigue
  • Nausea and vomiting

Cause & Spread of Malaria

  • Malaria is caused Plasmodium parasites.
  • Spread through bites of infected female Anopheles mosquitoes.

Prevention & Treatment of Malaria

  • Use insecticide-treated mosquito nets (ITNs).
  • Indoor residual spraying.
  • Take antimalarial prophylaxis if in high-risk areas.
  • Eliminate standing water to reduce mosquito breeding.
Treat ment can be done by taking antimalarial drugs (e.g., artemisinin-based combination therapies, or ACTs).
